[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Tears are falling like the midnight rain
But I am here to wash away the pain
Pull you closer in the candlelight
Hold your body through the restless night

[Verse 1]
Shadows dancing on the cabin wall
I can hear the gentle way you call
Lost inside a dream you cannot break
I will keep you safe until you wake

[Pre-Chorus]
Every shiver pulls you into my chest
Lay your weary heavy head down to rest
Skin on skin we leave the sorrow behind
You are the only peace I wanted to find

[Chorus]
Softly crying in your sleep tonight
Let me wrap you in the morning light
Feel the steady rhythm that we keep
No more softly crying in your sleep

[Post-Chorus]
[Whispered] (Let me hold you now)
No more crying in your sleep

[Verse 2]

[Whispered] (Let me hold you now)

[Bridge]
